From Silver Screen Escapism to Real-World Adventure

As movie lovers here at Theaterist, we're often captivated by the stunning landscapes and authentic adventures depicted on screen. Now, I'm thrilled to introduce a personal project born from that same spirit of exploration: Goraput.site.

My name is Marko, and Goraput ("Mountain Path") is my comprehensive guide to the breathtaking, film-worthy beauty of Montenegro—a hidden gem offering both "accessible luxury" and raw, "authentic adventure." If you've ever dreamt of stepping into those cinematic scenes, let Goraput be your trusted path.
